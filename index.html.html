July 14, 2012

I saw some Burma Shave style signs on the side of the road today: Passing cars, When you can’t see, May get you, A glimpse, Of eternity. I definitely won’t be passing any cars.

June 2, 2012

My first day of the trip! I can’t believe finally got everything packed and ready to go. Because I’m on a Segway, I wasn’t able to bring a whole lot with me:

  • cell phone,
  • iPod,
  • digital camera, and
  • a protein bar. Just the essentials. As Lao Tzu would have said, A journey of a thousand miles begins with one Segway.
